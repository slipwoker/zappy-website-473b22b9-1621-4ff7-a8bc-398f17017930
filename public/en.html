Language and Linguistics

Language and Dialect Studies

Research, articles and reference materials in the field of linguistics, dialects and linguistic diversity of the world's languages. The content is intended for researchers, teachers and anyone seeking to deepen their understanding of language as a cultural and social phenomenon.

Roots and Background

Cultural Memory and Heritage

תכנים על תרבויות, Roots and Background היסטורי — מסיפורי קהילות ועד ניתוח מנהגים ומסורות. הפלטפורמה מציעה מרחב לשיתוף ידע על תרבויות ושפות מרחבי העולם, תוך שמירה על עומק אקדמי ונגישות רחבה.

Religion and Tradition

Religious Traditions and Faith

Articles and stories about religions and cultures — from Abrahamic traditions to Eastern and folk beliefs. The content presents religion as a living cultural space, from a respectful, research-based approach that is open to interfaith dialogue.

Interpretation and Research

Interdisciplinary Reading

A unique approach connecting language, culture and religion as intertwined knowledge domains. Culturlex invites researchers and educators to share analyses, interpretations and reference materials that see the interface between disciplines as a source of deeper understanding of humanity.

Collections נבחרים

A digital archive of linguistic, cultural and religious knowledge — each collection presented as a curated record, with a commitment to accuracy, depth and accessibility. Here you will find lexicons, oral histories, ritual texts and teaching materials from the fields of language, culture and heritage.

Language and Linguistics

Multilingual Lexicons and Dictionaries

A repository of words, expressions and glossaries from dialects, vernaculars and endangered languages — a living documentation of human linguistic diversity.
